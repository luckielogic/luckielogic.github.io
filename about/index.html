Lucky is a six-year old good boy who originally came from the Californian desert. He currently resides in Oregon with his two humans. Lucky is a black labrador retriever mix, with a husky mind and coat, and a bit of (suspected) beagle thrown in as well.

Lucky loves: Belly rubs, rolling on the grass, barking, rawhide, snow, other friendly dogs, sleeping, and chasing small moving things.


Lucky may look cute and derpy, but don't be fooled. He thinks he is very smart. This website is a collection of his everyday thoughts and words of wisdom.

Lucky's name is spelled with a -y, but the domain name is taken. Thus, Luckie Logic was created.
